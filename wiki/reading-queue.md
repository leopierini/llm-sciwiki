# Reading Queue

Papers suggested based on identified gaps in the wiki.

## High Priority

- **Confirmatory study of baseline spindle density moderator** — Kumar (2025) found an exploratory interaction; a pre-registered confirmatory test is critical. Search: "sleep spindles baseline individual differences stimulation memory"
- **Meta-analysis of sleep spindles and memory** — Would elevate evidence to level 1 and help reconcile divergent findings. Search: "sleep spindles memory consolidation meta-analysis"
- **Spindle-memory studies in older adults** — Critical gap: all current sources used young adults only. Older adults have lower baseline spindle density, making them a natural test of the moderator hypothesis. Search: "sleep spindles memory aging elderly"

## Medium Priority

- **Procedural/emotional memory and spindles** — All sources used declarative (word-pair or face-name) tasks. Search: "sleep spindles procedural memory" or "sleep spindles emotional memory"
- **Long-term effects of spindle stimulation** — Rodriguez 2024 and Kumar 2025 were both single-night; multi-night protocols needed
- **Individual participant data meta-analysis** — Pooling raw data from Rodriguez (2024) and Kumar (2025) could test the baseline moderator more precisely
- **Mechanisms linking spindles to consolidation** — Hippocampal-cortical dialogue during spindles, thalamocortical models

## Low Priority

- **Alternative stimulation methods** — tDCS during sleep, pharmacological spindle enhancement (zolpidem) — would make a good comparison page
