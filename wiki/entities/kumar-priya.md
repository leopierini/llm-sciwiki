# Kumar, Priya

**Type:** Person (Researcher)

## Overview

Neuroscientist who led the multi-site replication attempt of the spindle stimulation-memory effect (Kumar, 2025). The study replicated the physiological enhancement of spindles but failed to replicate the downstream memory benefit, significantly shaping the current controversy around spindle stimulation efficacy.

## Key Contributions

- Led pre-registered, multi-site replication of Rodriguez (2024) — see [[sources/kumar-2025-spindle-replication]]
- Identified potential baseline spindle density moderator (exploratory)
