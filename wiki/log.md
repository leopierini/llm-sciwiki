## [2024-03-16] ingest | Kumar et al. 2025 — Multi-site replication: spindle enhancement replicated, memory benefit not
Third source ingested. Pre-registered, multi-site RCT (n=204) that replicated spindle enhancement (d=0.88) but NOT memory improvement (d=0.18, BF01=4.2). Exploratory subgroup analysis suggests the memory benefit may be limited to individuals with low baseline spindle density. This contradicts Rodriguez (2024) and triggers a confidence downgrade of the stimulation-memory claim from moderate to contested.
